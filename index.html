Gabriel Brown is a doctoral graduate student in the Mechanical Science and Enginering Department at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. He graduated from the University of Notre Dame in 2020 with a degree in Mechanical Engineering.
His main academic interests are multiscale and multiphysics modeling, as well as computational science and numerical methods.
